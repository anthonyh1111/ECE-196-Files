Problem Definition

Student-accessible parking on the UCSD campus is not optimal for the large number of students that use these parking spaces.

Problem Motivation

Why this matters to the team

Our team chose this issue because resource overload is a real student experience: support exists, but finding the right option at the right time is confusing. We want to better understand that gap before proposing any new idea.

Current Existing Solutions

How people currently navigate the problem

University resource directories

Students browse official campus websites that list tutoring, advising, counseling, and emergency resources.

  • Great for credibility and completeness
  • Can feel overwhelming when someone needs a quick answer

Peer recommendations

Students often rely on friends, class group chats, or clubs to find help.

  • Fast and relatable
  • Advice may be inconsistent or incomplete

Search engines and social platforms

Some students search broadly online when they do not know the exact office or service name.

  • Easy starting point
  • Results may be outdated, generic, or not campus-specific
